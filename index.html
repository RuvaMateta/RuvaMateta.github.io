01 — Introduction

About me

I'm a 21-year-old Biotechnology & Bioinformatics student from Harare, Zimbabwe, with a growing focus on computational genomics and applied data science. I'm currently a remote research intern studying genetic variation and the evolution of tomato flavor and nutrition, and I work on-site at the National Pathology Research and Diagnostic Centre Zimbabwe.

Alongside my research, I completed the Aspire Leaders Program (Cohort 2) and am now part of its Extended Leadership Program, while actively participating in the Mastuo Lab Data Science Program — building my skills at the intersection of biology, data, and leadership.

I'm drawn to problems where wet-lab biology meets computation: understanding what a genome is doing, and building the workflows to actually find out.
